1. Business Case ▼

Field workers need to create T&M work orders on-site, from their phones, at the end of a shift or when extra work is performed. This is the single most critical mobile feature for T&M workflow adoption. Without it, field data capture falls back to paper forms and WhatsApp photos — the exact problem Appello is solving.

Every customer interviewed uses some form of mobile field entry today — whether it's Clearstory's app, a custom form library, or paper. Appello must provide a native, purpose-built mobile WO creation experience.

Customer Evidence

"The work order has been submitted from the field. They go into the form library, grab the work order template, answer the questions, and submit. It's clunky but it works." — Tear Systems
"Our supervisors use the form library as a workaround. They fill out the T&M form with a supervisor PIN for sign-off. We need a proper work order flow." — Pedersen Corp
"T&M form accessible via mobile app for field workers to fill out at end of shift. That's the core requirement." — AB Insulation

2. Scope ▼

In Scope

  • IN 6-step mobile form: Date/Info, Labor, Materials, Equipment, Photos, Signatures
  • IN SelectionSlideup pickers for TradeLevel, Material, and Equipment selection
  • IN Camera-based photo capture (up to 5 photos per WO)
  • IN Touch-based signature canvas for worker and optional customer signatures
  • IN Draft management — save and resume multiple concurrent drafts
  • IN Auto-pricing based on selected trade levels and hour types
  • IN Supervisor notification on WO submission

Out of Scope

  • OUT Full offline mode with background sync (Phase 2)
  • OUT Barcode/QR scanning for materials
  • OUT Voice-to-text for description fields

4. Data Model ▼

No new Prisma schema changes are required. The mobile form consumes the same work order models established in WO-1 (Job with jobType: WORK_ORDER), WO-2 (WorkOrderLineItem), and WO-3 (WorkOrderSignature, Asset for photos).

Shared Models Used

ModelSourceMobile Usage
JobWO-1Work order record (jobType = WORK_ORDER)
WorkOrderLineItemWO-2Labor, material, and equipment line items
WorkOrderSignatureWO-3Worker and customer signatures
AssetExistingPhoto attachments via createAsset mutation
TradeLevelExistingTrade level picker for labor items

7. Acceptance Criteria ▼

  • User can navigate to work order creation from the mobile app and see a 6-step guided form.
  • Progress bar updates correctly as user advances through steps.
  • SelectionSlideup pickers for trade level, material, and equipment display correct options from the API.
  • Labor item auto-calculates total based on selected trade level rates and entered hours (ST/OT/DT).
  • User can capture up to 5 photos using the device camera and see thumbnails in the grid.
  • Touch-based signature canvas captures worker signature (required) and optional customer signature.
  • User can save a draft at any step and resume it later; multiple concurrent drafts are supported.
  • On submission, a notification is sent to the designated supervisor.
  • Form handles validation errors inline (required fields, minimum 1 labor item).
